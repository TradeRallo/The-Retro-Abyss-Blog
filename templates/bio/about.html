About Retro Abyss

Retro Abyss is a personal creative project that began in 2008. It represents a fusion of music, visual art, and experimental ideas, forming a unique digital world.

The project was created as a space for self-expression — a place where thoughts, emotions, and concepts could exist without restrictions.

Status: Ongoing | Origin: 2008

Author

The project is developed by Saigotsuki Reiga — a fictional persona representing the creative identity behind Retro Abyss.

Through this character, the author explores themes of isolation, transformation, and artistic growth.

Philosophy

Retro Abyss is not just a blog — it is a timeline of evolution. Each entry, track, or artwork is a fragment of a larger narrative.

Imperfection is part of the process. Early works remain unchanged to preserve authenticity and the feeling of that moment in time.
